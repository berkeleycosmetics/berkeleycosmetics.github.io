Krasa Cosmetics Team


We are a student-run group out of UC Berkeley focusing on cosmetic innovation.

What is Krasa?
Krasa is the Czech word for beauty. Our team members liked how the word sounded, both elegant but slightly edgy. Our team hopes to capture the essence of the word, by creating a community to showcase the latest cosmetic trends, and also innovate high-end cosmetic products to be affordable and culturally diverse.



Cosmetics have largely traditionally been limited to certain shades and colors, but at Krasa, we don't believe in these limitations. We believe that people should be given the freedom to be creative with their cosmetic decisions, and hope to innovate products that reflect that.
